0% PLASTIC - THE HEALTHY CHOICE

CHAVA IS 100% WATER HYACINTH BIOMASS

The beginning of this natural container start with that during the COVID-19 situation, we like to order Food delivery so much, because it answers New Normal Life. But we have found that during this period the rate of plastic usage has increased, since in one order there are at least 6-8 pieces of plastic. Which affects the environment and the world a lot; it causes climate change from plastic waste disposal improperly. We know that we cannot stop the current of Food delivery, so we want to make the industry more sustainable for our lovely earthly friends.



OBSERVE WITH HEART

We were looking for natural materials that will help solve the problem. Then we found water hyacinth, which is considered a type of weed that affects ecosystems and aquatic life. It may seem useless, but when brought through the processing, we found it has some properties that are very well suited to being the main material of our utensils: shockproof, leakproof, and good heat and cold storage. Who would have thought, that one day these overlooked plants were going to become the heroes that would save the world.

From the empathy of the plastic waste combined with the ecological balance. Give us a rise to a commitment; to create things that are friendly to both people and nature. These are why "Chava" is founded, we are a brand of natural containers, with the hope to help reduce problems, and raise awareness of the value of the environment around everyone.

We created chava to make the most sustainable packaging in the most ethical way possible. All our products are 100% natural, compostable and biodegradable.
